SAFE AND FREE EXPRESSION

Without free expression, there can be no free thought.

SAFE exists to...

...protect free expression against all who seek to suppress it. Historically, governments have been the main offenders against free expression. However, in recent times, authoritarian groups and individuals have begun harnessing private companies to suppress views that they disagree with.

...preserve free expression by encouraging these companies to keep their platforms open for all, even in the face of social opposition. By keeping the exchange of ideas open and preserving free thought.

...uphold this key cornerstone of our society.
